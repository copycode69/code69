Frequently Asked Questions

During the recruitment and interview process, you can expect several stages including application review, initial screening, technical interviews, and final interviews. Each stage is designed to assess your skills, experience, and fit for the role.

After submitting your job application, it will be reviewed by the hiring team. If your qualifications match the requirements, you will be contacted for further steps which may include an initial screening or interview.

To apply for an internship or co-op position at Blurrock, visit our careers page, search for available positions, and submit your application through our online portal.

You can check the status of your application by logging into your account on our careers portal. Updates on your application status will also be sent to your registered email address.

Signing up for job notifications ensures that you stay informed about new job opportunities that match your preferences. You will receive alerts directly to your email, helping you to apply early and stay ahead in the recruitment process.
